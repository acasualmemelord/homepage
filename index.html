College projects

The Naming Game (Github)

The Naming Game is an experiment investigating agent dynamics of naming objects: can a group of agents autonomously agree upon a name without the influence of a central authority? In a group of four engaged in Agile development, I coded the backend of a program simulating this question.

Infinite Golf (Github)

Smogon projects

Smogon is a competitive Pokemon community with hundreds of thousands of users playing on Pokemon Showdown, an online battling simulator. I have used this site for more than 8 years and am a regular member of a specific subcommunity, Pet Mods, dedicated to creating new metagames by modifying Pokemon and mechanics.

DragonHeaven server (Github)

This is a side server which hosts all of the Pet Mods metagames. I have contributed almost 800 commits over 3+ years and coded more than a dozen different metagames.
